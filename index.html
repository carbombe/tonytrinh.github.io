About Me

Hi my name is Thien Trinh, but people usually call me Tony and that's what I like to go by! I am adventurous kind of guy, I would one day love to travel around the world to experience all kinds of things. My work ethic is strong meaning that I take attendance, character, communication, productivity, respect, and teamwork very seriously. That doesn't mean that I am a difficult person though, I occasionally joke around during work. Outside of work I am very laxed and would always love to be around the people I love. My hobbies and free time killers are playing computer games (League, Overwatch, Valorant, and etc.), as well as working out!

I am an Arizona State University alumni with a Bachelor's of Science in Psychology. During my undergrad years, I was able to take a Human Factors class and was very excited about the topic, which is why I want to go into User Experience! But with the experience I have with research and writing lab reports, I've decided to go towards the "Research" route. I have a knack for interacting with people and observing their behaviors. Seeing the thought process that goes into many designs around the world and the way people interact with them excites me the most! Like why are keyboards designed the way they are designed? I actually know that answer due to a report I wrote so you can ask me about it.

*psst* I know this may sound weird but turning people's actions and behaviors into data is super cool to me.

Experience

To be honest, other than the Career Foundry bootcamp I took, a solo design project that I've made (Zipp), hours and hours of UX videos I've watched, 3 years of research, and many many UX books and articles I've read, I don't really have any actual UX Research experience with a company. Which is why I hope to land an internship or an entry level job that allows me to bring my many skills I have to use!
